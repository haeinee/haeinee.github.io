Overview

Objective:
This app helps users to go low waste and eco-friendlier lifestyle by providing blog posts and selling zero waste products.

Context:
There are few apps that educate users how to recycle, organize and manage their freezers. But there are no apps that support informative article or shopping.

Users:
This app will be designed for people who are pursuing low waste and eco-friendly living.

Key Features:
This app would allow users to read the articles and blog posts regarding low waste living, and to shop reusable and package-free products.
